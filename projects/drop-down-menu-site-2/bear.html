BEAR

Bear is a 1 Year old Liger Cub!!
Bear is saved from a zoo facility, that specialized in testing animals. Bear seems to be lonely, and would really like a family to feed him, and take him on long walks within our wildlife facility.
Being once a lab experiment, he is now free to roam, and love as he pleases, in search for a pride to call his own. Bear also has a dissability where his wildlife instincts, changed him more into a big house cat. He loves cuddles! with a supervisor on hand of course, then after a year of getting to know you , you can tend to him without supervision.
