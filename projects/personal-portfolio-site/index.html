About Me

My Name is Sahlina Jenkins, I am a new up incoming Graphic Illustrator who does a bit of web design on the side. I have had a passion for art as long as I could remember. After many years of trial and error, going to different schools, I finally attended a school which motivated me and inspired my art to grow in a whole other direction. Being a single mother also helped grow my art, not to make it a whole sop story, my child inspires more art and inspiration. My goal is to share my art, and Ideas with you the viewer. In hope that my art will reach you, into which you will reach out to me. I hope you enjoy my many multi-joys.

-Thank You
